How It Works

The Inspection Process, Step by Step

We make the home inspection process simple, transparent, and stress-free. Here's what to expect when you book with Potter Home Inspections of Maine.

1

Schedule Your Inspection

Call us or use our online booking tool below. We confirm a date and time that works around your closing schedule — often within 24–48 hours.

2

On-Site Inspection Day

Matthew personally conducts a thorough 2–4 hour inspection. You're encouraged to attend — we'll walk you through every finding live and in person.

3

Receive Your Report

A clear, detailed digital report with photos and descriptions of all findings — typically delivered the same day as your inspection.

4

Review and Ask Questions

Matthew is always available to clarify findings, explain priorities, and help you understand what's truly important before you close.
