What We Offer

Comprehensive Home Inspection Services

From buyer inspections to pre-listing evaluations, Potter Home Inspections covers every aspect of the home — so you can make confident, informed decisions.

01

Buyer's Home Inspection

The most important step before closing. We conduct a comprehensive top-to-bottom inspection covering structural components, mechanical systems, roofing, foundation, electrical, plumbing, and more.

02

Pre-Listing Inspection

Selling your home? Identify and address issues before they become deal-breakers. Sellers who inspect early reduce negotiation surprises and often sell faster and at asking price.

03

New Construction Inspection

Even new builds have deficiencies. We inspect new construction homes at key phases and upon completion to ensure your builder has met standards before you take ownership.

04

11-Month Warranty Inspection

If your new home came with a 1-year builder's warranty, an 11-month inspection ensures you identify defects before your warranty expires — protecting you from costly out-of-pocket repairs.

05

Radon Testing

Maine has elevated radon levels in many regions. We offer professional radon testing to assess your home's air quality and ensure your family is safe from this odorless, radioactive gas.

06

Annual Home Maintenance Inspection

Proactive annual inspections help you catch small issues before they become expensive problems. Think of it as a routine check-up for your home's long-term health.
